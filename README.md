## 📌 About ICODS

ICODS is dedicated to integrating behavioral science and data analytics to drive evidence-based decision-making, public policy improvement, and behavioral compliance.

### Core Areas of Expertise
- **Behavioral Systems Analysis (BSA)** & Organizational Design
- **Data Analytics** applied to Social Sciences & Public Policy
- **Behavioral Compliance** & Cultural Change Programs
- **Evidence-based Evaluation** of Public & Educational Initiatives
